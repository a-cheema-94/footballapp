Common Event Types:

Mouse Events: Used for mouse interactions (click, mouseover, etc.).
MouseEvent<HTMLButtonElement>

Keyboard Events: Used for keyboard interactions (keydown, keyup, etc.).
KeyboardEvent<HTMLInputElement>

Form Events: Used for form interactions (submit, change, etc.).
FormEvent<HTMLFormElement>
ChangeEvent<HTMLInputElement>

Focus Events: Used for focus interactions (focus, blur)
FocusEvent<HTMLInputElement>

Drag Events: Used for drag interactions (dragstart, dragend, etc.).
DragEvent<HTMLDivElement>
